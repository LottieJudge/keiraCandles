About Me

Hi, I’m Keira, and my love for candles led me to start this business! I’ve always loved the cosy, calming atmosphere that candles create, but buying them online can be expensive. That’s why I started making my own, bringing affordability, creativity, and quality together.

For me, candle-making is more than just a business. It’s a way to share my passion with others. I love being able to customise each candle, allowing you to choose the perfect scent and design to match your space. My candles are made with soy wax and essential oils, making them natural, vegan-friendly, and better for the environment.

More than anything, I want my candles to bring a sense of peace and positivity into your home. Life can be stressful, and I hope my candles help create a relaxing, calming atmosphere for you to enjoy.

Thank you for supporting my small business and being part of my journey!
